# Case Study

## Challenge

A global enterprise struggled with duplicate content, inconsistent metadata, low discoverability, and fragmented ownership.

## Solution

- Introduced governance framework
- Standardized metadata
- Built taxonomy
- Implemented quality checks
- Created analytics dashboards
- Introduced AI-assisted editorial workflows

## Outcomes

- Improved content quality
- Faster publishing
- Better discoverability
- Increased content reuse
- Stronger governance
